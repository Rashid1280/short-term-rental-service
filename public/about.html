About Our Rental Service

This website is a short-term rental service designed especially for people visiting Raipur for treatment or procedures at AIIMS. Many times, the medical process can take weeks or even months, making it difficult for patients and their families to find affordable, short-term stays near the hospital. Our platform solves this problem by connecting people with budget-friendly rental options close to AIIMS, so they can focus on their health and comfort without worrying about expensive accommodations.
